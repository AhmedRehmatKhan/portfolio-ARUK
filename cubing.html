About WCA

World Cube Association

The World Cube Association (WCA) is the worldwide non-profit organization that regulates and holds competitions for mechanical puzzles that are operated by twisting groups of pieces, commonly known as 'twisty puzzles'.

The WCA was founded in 2004 and has since grown to oversee thousands of competitions worldwide, maintaining official world records and rankings for speedcubing events.

Cubing Tutorials

Speedcubing Methods

CFOP Method

The most popular speedcubing method. Learn Cross, F2L, OLL, and PLL.

Cross: Solve the bottom cross
F2L: First Two Layers
OLL: Orient Last Layer
PLL: Permute Last Layer

ZBLL Method

Advanced method for last layer solving with 493 algorithms.

Prerequisite: Know full OLL
Cases: 493 algorithms
Benefit: Skip PLL step
Level: Advanced

Roux Method

Block-building method focusing on efficiency and fewer moves.

Block 1: Left 1x2x3 block
Block 2: Right 1x2x3 block
CMLL: Corners of last layer
LSE: Last Six Edges
